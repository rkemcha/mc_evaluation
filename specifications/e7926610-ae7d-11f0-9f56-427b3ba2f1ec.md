### 10. Content Description
This microcredential is composed of four distinct modules that guide the learner from theory to application.
*   **Module 1: Foundations of AI in CSCL:** Explores the theoretical underpinnings of Computer-Supported Collaborative Learning and identifies key opportunities and challenges for AI-driven support.
*   **Module 2: AI Techniques for Educational Data:** Covers core technical skills in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML) for analyzing educational data, with a focus on discussion transcripts and interaction logs.
*   **Module 3: Advanced Learning Analytics for CSCL:** Delves into advanced analytical methods, including Social Network Analysis (SNA) and multimodal data analysis, to generate deep insights into group dynamics and knowledge co-construction.
*   **Module 4: Capstone Project - Design, Build, Evaluate:** A cumulative project where learners apply their knowledge to design, implement, and ethically evaluate a novel AI-powered prototype for a real-world collaborative learning scenario.

### 15. Learning Activities
Learning is achieved through a combination of theoretical study and practical application:
*   **Research Reviews:** Critical analysis of seminal and recent academic papers.
