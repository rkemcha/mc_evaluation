### **8. Admission Requirements**

### **9. Learning Objectives**
This microcredential provides learners with the advanced theoretical knowledge and practical skills to design, build, and deploy sophisticated artificial intelligence systems. It focuses on modern deep learning architectures and the operational lifecycle of machine learning models.

### **10. Content Description**
The curriculum is structured into modules covering the end-to-end machine learning lifecycle:
- **Module 1: Foundations of Deep Learning:** Advanced review of neural network fundamentals, training techniques, and performance metrics.
- **Module 2: Computer Vision with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs):** Architectures and applications for image analysis.
- **Module 3: Sequence Modeling with Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs):** Architectures for time-series and sequential data.
- **Module 4: Transformer Architectures and Generative AI:** In-depth study of self-attention mechanisms, Transformers, and their application in Large Language Models (LLMs).
- **Module 5: MLOps & Model Deployment:** Principles of Machine Learning Operations, including model containerization with Docker and strategies for deployment to production environments.
- **Module 6: Capstone Project:** A substantial project requiring the development, containerization, and deployment of a complete AI application.

### **11. Mode of Instruction**
- **Mode:** Blended Learning (Part-time)
- **Description:** The course combines asynchronous online lectures and self-paced materials with synchronous online Q&A sessions and mandatory in-person, hands-on lab sessions to ensure practical skill development.

### **12. Learning Outcomes**
Upon successful completion of this microcredential, the learner will be able to:
- **LO1:** Critically evaluate and select appropriate machine learning and deep learning models for complex problems.
- **LO2:** Design and implement advanced neural network architectures, including Transformer-based models for Generative AI tasks, using Python and relevant libraries.
- **LO3:** Apply sophisticated techniques for model training, optimization, and regularization to improve performance.
- **LO4:** Develop, containerize, and deploy a complete AI application that solves a real-world problem, documenting the deployment strategy.
- **LO5:** Analyze the ethical implications and potential biases in AI models and datasets.

### **18. Assessment Methods**
Assessment is a combination of continuous assessment and final examinations, weighted as follows:
- **Programming Assignments (30%):** Three assignments designed to build and reinforce practical coding skills.
- **Final Project (45%):** A substantial project requiring the development of an AI application using a Transformer-based model, a comprehensive report including deployment and ethical considerations, a final presentation, and a containerized version of the model.
- **Written Examination (25%):** A final exam covering theoretical concepts and problem-solving.
