### 9. Learning Objectives
Upon successful completion of this microcredential, learners will gain the expertise to:
*   Critically assess the ethical, pedagogical, and technical impact of AI systems in educational environments.
*   Design and build functional prototypes of Generative AI-powered educational tools using modern APIs and prompt engineering techniques.
*   Apply fundamental machine learning methods to analyze educational data and derive actionable learning analytics insights.
*   Synthesize and critique cutting-edge research to inform evidence-based practices and drive future innovation in the AI in Education field.

### 10. Content Description
The microcredential is structured into four core modules:
*   **Module 1: Foundations of AI in Education:** Explores the history, key concepts, and pedagogical implications of AIEd. A strong focus is placed on critically evaluating ethical frameworks, data privacy, and algorithmic bias in educational contexts.
*   **Module 2: Learning Analytics and Machine Learning:** Provides hands-on experience in using Python and core data science libraries to analyze educational datasets. Learners will apply fundamental machine learning techniques to identify patterns and generate insights.
*   **Module 3: Generative AI & LLMs for Education:** A deep dive into the architecture and application of Large Language Models (LLMs). This module includes practical labs on interacting with LLM APIs, principles of effective prompt engineering, and designing AI-powered tutors and content generators.
*   **Module 4: AIEd Prototype Capstone Project:** A culminating project where learners design, build, and document a functional Generative AI-powered educational prototype that addresses a specific learning challenge.

### 11. Mode of Instruction
