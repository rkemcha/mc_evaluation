## 9. Learning Objectives:
* Understand the key concepts and technologies related to Smart Cities.
* Analyze the challenges and opportunities in developing and implementing Smart City solutions.
* Design and develop innovative solutions for urban problems using data and technology.
* Evaluate the ethical and societal implications of Smart City initiatives.

## 10. Content Description:
* **Module 1: Introduction to Smart Cities:** Overview of Smart City concepts, trends, and challenges.
* **Module 2: Smart City Technologies:** Exploration of key technologies such as IoT, data analytics, AI, and cloud computing.
* **Module 3: Smart City Applications:** Case studies of Smart City applications in areas such as transportation, energy, healthcare, and public safety.
* **Module 4: Smart City Design and Implementation:**  Principles and methods for designing and implementing Smart City solutions.
* **Module 5: Smart City Ethics and Governance:**  Ethical considerations and governance frameworks for Smart City initiatives.

## 12. Learning Outcomes:

* Design and develop Smart City solutions using appropriate technologies.
* Analyze the ethical and societal impacts of Smart City initiatives.
* Evaluate the performance and effectiveness of Smart City deployments.
* Communicate effectively about Smart City concepts and technologies.
