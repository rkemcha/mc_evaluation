### **10. Content Description**
This expert-level microcredential explores the integration of Artificial Intelligence into Computer-Supported Collaborative Learning (CSCL) environments. Learners will critically evaluate theoretical frameworks, analyze ethical implications, and gain hands-on experience in designing and prototyping next-generation, Generative AI-driven tools to support and analyze collaborative learning processes.

**Modules Include:**
- **Module 1: Foundations of CSCL and AI in Education:** Exploring core theories of collaborative learning and the history of AI applications in educational contexts.
- **Module 2: AI Techniques for Analyzing Collaboration:** Hands-on labs focusing on educational data mining and learning analytics to model and understand group dynamics.
- **Module 3: Generative AI for Collaborative Learning:** A deep dive into Large Language Models (LLMs), prompt engineering, and fine-tuning for creating AI-powered tutors, facilitators, and collaborative agents.
- **Module 4: Ethical AI and Research Design in EdTech:** Focusing on bias, fairness, and transparency in AI systems, and developing a research proposal to test an AI-for-CSCL intervention.
