### **9. Learning Objectives**
Upon successful completion, learners will be able to:
1.  Critically analyze new national occupational safety standards and translate them into strategic organizational objectives.
2.  Lead advanced risk assessments (e.g., HAZOP, LOPA) for complex industrial processes impacted by new regulations.
3.  Design and implement a comprehensive change management program, **grounded in the principles of psychological safety,** to embed new safety protocols and foster a proactive reporting culture.
4.  Evaluate the effectiveness of safety interventions using both leading and lagging indicators.
5.  Develop and defend a strategic, evidence-based implementation plan for a new safety standard to a senior management audience.

### **10. Content Description**
The program is structured into four integrated modules, culminating in a capstone project.
*   **Module 1: The Regulatory Landscape & Strategic Interpretation:** Deep dive into the legal and ethical framework of new standards, with a specific unit dedicated to the host nation's regulatory context (e.g., Germany's ArbSchG). Focus on policy analysis and strategic alignment with business goals.
*   **Module 2: Advanced Risk Management & Control Systems:** Practical application of advanced hazard identification and risk assessment techniques. Includes hands-on workshops with industry-standard software.
*   **Module 3: Leading Organizational Change & Safety Culture:** Focuses on the human element of safety. Begins with a foundational unit on **Psychological Safety** as the bedrock for EHS excellence. Covers change management models, communication strategies, and methods for improving safety culture.
*   **Module 4: Capstone Project - Safety Standard Implementation Plan:** A semester-long project where learners develop a comprehensive, real-world implementation plan for a new standard.

### **11. Mode of Instruction**
*   **Blended Learning:** A mix of asynchronous online learning (pre-recorded lectures, readings) following a Flipped Classroom approach, and synchronous virtual/in-person sessions for interactive workshops, case study discussions, and hands-on simulation labs.

*   **Lead:** Manage organizational change effectively by fostering psychological safety and developing a proactive safety culture.
