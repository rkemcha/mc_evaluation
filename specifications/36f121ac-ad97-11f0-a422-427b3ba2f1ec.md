### 9. Learning Objectives
Upon successful completion of this microcredential, learners will be able to:
1.  Critically evaluate the ethical implications, risks, and opportunities of using predictive models in educational contexts.
2.  Apply techniques to identify, measure, and mitigate bias in student datasets and machine learning models.
3.  Build, train, and tune predictive models (e.g., logistic regression, random forests) using fairness-aware machine learning techniques.
4.  Evaluate models using a comprehensive suite of metrics, including both traditional performance metrics (e.g., accuracy, F1-score) and fairness-aware metrics (e.g., demographic parity, equalized odds).
5.  Develop a robust privacy-by-design and ethical implementation plan for a real-world learning analytics project.

### 10. Content Description
This microcredential provides a deep dive into the technical and ethical dimensions of predictive modeling for student success. Learners will progress through the entire modeling pipeline, from ethical data sourcing and preparation to building, evaluating, and planning the deployment of models. The curriculum places a strong emphasis on responsible AI, teaching participants how to create models that are not only accurate but also fair, transparent, and respectful of student privacy.

-   **Module 1: Foundations of Predictive Modeling:** Introduction to the learning analytics workflow and core modeling concepts.
-   **Module 2: Ethical AI, Privacy, and Fairness in Education:** A deep dive into ethical frameworks, data privacy regulations (GDPR), and the principles of fairness in an educational context.
-   **Module 3: Data Wrangling & Bias Detection:** Hands-on techniques for data cleaning, feature engineering, and using statistical methods to identify potential bias in student datasets.
-   **Module 4: Building Fair & Interpretable Models:** Practical application of fairness-aware algorithms and techniques to train and tune models that are both predictive and equitable.
-   **Module 5: Advanced Evaluation & Responsible Implementation:** Focus on evaluating models using a dual lens of performance and fairness, culminating in the development of a comprehensive, ethical implementation plan.
